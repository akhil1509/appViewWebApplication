ROBO BUCKET CHALLENGE




DESCRIPTION
 Robo Bucket Challenge is the event consist of balls (Different Colours) with a Wheeled robot (Wireless controlled) on the Table. In a Team, one Participant should control the robot and manage to get down the balls from the table with the help of robot .other participant has to manage to collect the dropping balls in a Bucket before they reach the ground. Do’s and Don’ts:
1. The table contains the inner boundary marked and the robot should not cross the inner boundary. If the inner boundary is crossed the team is out.
2. Balls should be dropped only with the help of robot. If any malpractice is noticed, the team will be disqualified.
3. Hands should not be used while collecting the balls i.e. the balls must fall into the bucket without any external agent.
Points:
Points are awarded based on the colour and number of balls collected in the bucket. The points for each colour are:
1. Red Ball-5 points
2. Green Ball-4 points
3. Orange Ball-3 points
4. Blue Ball-2 points
5. Black Ball- the team is out if the black ball is collected in the bucket.
Team size: 2 members
Certificate of participation to everyone who participated.
Registration fee: 100/- (per team)

NOTE:
Proper care has to be taken while operating the Robot. If any damage caused the participants will be charged.
