JUDGING CRITERIA : No. of images they wrote correct in right order in given time; One who does all counters fast and correct.
DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
The contestants will be shown a series of images on slides one by one. Each slide will be shown for 3 seconds. The number of slides will be 15, after it the participants are to write the images in order as shown previously within a time limit of 30 seconds. The shortlisted participants will be qualified to next round based on the number of correct answers in least time.
ROUND-2:
The contestants will be shown part a and part b for 15 seconds which should be matched correctly Meanwhile the contestants have to write the correct order. The shortlisted participants are qualified to next round.
ROUND-3:
There will be 3 counters Counter1 Participants should solve a jigsaw puzzle Counter2 Participants will be given a task to stick the symbols of periodic elements given on cardboard sheet with piece of paper where symbols are written priorly Counter3 Participants will be given a riddle which they need to solve. Clues will be given if needed.
