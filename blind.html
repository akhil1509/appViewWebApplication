Blind Fold




COST : 30/-
To register for the event contact Help desk at GRIET and Spot registrations are available
JUDGING CRITERIA : Based on coding the programme
DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
The participants will be given basic simple questions for which they have to write a programme in ‘C’ when the laptop monitors are covered. A programme will be shown for 1 min and the participant will have to type it, laptop is covered with a cloth. The participants who answer correctly in least time will be shortlisted to next round
ROUND-2:
Participants will be given keywords in jumbled manner. Participants should sort out them and implement those keywords to form a meaningful program. First one compile & execute is declared as winner
