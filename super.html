Super hero mania




COST : 30/-
To register for the event contact Help desk at GRIET.
Spot registrations are available

DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
Participants will be given 25 questions on general Superhero topics. The team which answers maximum number of questions will be qualified next round.

ROUND-2:
1. Picture quiz:
Various pictures of Superheroes & villains will be displayed and the participants have to guess the hero or the villain 2. Rapid fire round:
Questions will be asked to each team based on the general superhero topics and the team which answers maximum number of questions in least time will be qualified to the final round .

ROUND-3:
Team will be given a crossword to solve on Superheroes followed by an Audio visual round. The participants with the highest points will be declared as winners.
