IDEATHON




DESCRIPTION
 The Ideathon aims to showcase the best of regional entrepreneurs with great idea. We believe that an idea that is innovative is worth pursuing. We believe in ‘Possibilities’ and hence, we have created a platform called ‘Ideathon’. If you have an innovative idea and are looking at taking it to the next level, we invite you to participate in ‘Ideathon’ Challenge.
Submit your idea to the Ideathon.
Eligibility Criteria
The Idea has to be in any topic.
The Idea has to provide their current, complete and accurate information like PPT.
The Idea has to Design structure using Technology.
The idea must be original and should not be copied in either full or part.
The Idea should not be a functional start-up.
Time for the development of an idea.
Output of the idea, how to reduce the problem of an idea with solution.
The scope of the idea.
Judging will be based on the following criteria:
Value proposition, Target market, Competitive advantage, Business model and Innovation.
Based on the results, FUNDING will be provided to the “BEST IDEA”.
Team size: 1to 3 max
Certificate of participation to everyone who participated.
Registration fee: per Team 100/-
