JUDGING CRITERIA : One who answers all the questions accurately in least time.
DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
In this round 20 basic electric symbols regarding EEE department will be asked. Participants who answer maximum number of questions in the least time will be short listed to next round.
ROUND-2:
In this round participants will be given circuits with mistakes/errors. Participants will have to identify the mistakes in the circuits and those who identify maximum number of errors in a given time will be short listed to next round.
ROUND-3:
In this round, multiple values of resistors will be given to the participants. The participants have to pick the resistors from the box and need find the accurate resistance to the given value in least amount of time will be declared as the winners.
