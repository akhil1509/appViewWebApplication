JUDGING CRITERIA : Based on the number of correctly answered questions.
DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
Word Search. An eight-by-eight matrix consists of alphabets from which the participant has to choose the related terminology or words related to bio-medical equipment’s etc, The shortlisted participants will be qualified to next round.
ROUND-2:
Organ/Medical equipment’s will be displayed. The related disease, Organ and equipment should be described and written. The shortlisted participants will be qualified to next round.
ROUND-3:
30-40 images are displayed once in a particular order and the participants need to write the names of images in order after the display of it is done. Based on the number of correct answers in least time the winner is decided.
