JUDGING CRITERIA : Based on maximum number of answers in least time.
DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
Basic technical questions are given to participants. Whoever answers the maximum no. of questions in the given time will be qualified to the next round.
ROUND-2:
Here pictures based on basic electronic elements will be shown to the participants. The team has to answer the puzzle based on the picture. Whoever answers the maximum number of puzzles will qualified to final round.
ROUND-3:
: In this round, 10 slides are displayed for 7 seconds each and will be turned off. The team that answers the maximum number of questions in reverse order based on the slides within a time limit minds will be the winners.
