JUDGING CRITERIA : Based of maximum no of questions answered in given time.
DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
(i) The Participants should find technical words from a word puzzle. They should answer 20 questions in 10 mins (ii) The Participants should find technical words from a cross word. They should answer 16 questions in 10 mins Participants who answer maximum no. of questions in a least time are shortlisted to next round.
ROUND-2:
It is a quiz round where the teams are asked a few number of questions and the participants are allowed to choose a multiplier before the question. Based on the selected multiplier the points of the participants will be awarded (or) reduced. Participants with maximum number of points in least time will be qualified to next round Example: If participants select X2 then if the question is answered, the participants get 20 points .
