JUDGING CRITERIA : Based on number of correctly answered questions.
DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
This round includes quiz of 25 multiple choice questions based on surveying which should be completed in 30 minutes. Based on number of correct answers in least time, cut-off will be decided and more than 5 teams will be qualified to round-2.
ROUND-2:
The qualified teams are given dumpy/auto levels to take the reduced levels of certain points. The team which completes the levelling in least time with more accurate levels will be declared as winner.
