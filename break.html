Break The Rule




COST : 30/-
To register for the event contact Help desk at GRIET.
Spot registrations are available.
JUDGING CRITERIA : Based on the number of correctly answered technical words in least.
DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
: A Slide displaying a few pictures will be shown to the participants for “15 sec”. The participants should guess the connection between the pictures and note down the exact technical word/words in the given sheet. The shortlisted participants are qualified to next round.
ROUND-2:
To each team a slip will be given. In the slip, a Riddle and a Clue will be given. The riddle will be used for Next round but the Clue is used in this round. The participants should solve the clue to find out the next slip. The shortlisted participants are qualified to next round.
ROUND-3:
The riddle in each slip should be solved by the participants. Arrange the slips in the step-wise manner and the participants should come to the volunteers/co-ordinator. We will explain the protocol to the participants and provide them the needed equipment. The team should perform the experiment.
