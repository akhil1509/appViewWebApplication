JUDGING CRITERIA : Based on maximum number of questions answered in least time.
DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
It is a pen and paper round. 20 questions will be given. Two clues will be given for each question and participant have to solve them in a given time limit. The short listed candidates will be qualified to next round.
ROUND-2:
4 questions and 30 small chits will be displayed. In this 30 chits, 5 chits are related to each one of the question and 10 chits are included in them for confusion. Participant will have to pick up 5 chits related to each question. The one who completes it in the short span of time will be the winner.
