DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
The first round has two sections, in the first section 16 logos are given to the team and they have to match the logo to its correct answer. In second section 14 logos will be given, and the team has to guess the correct brand.

ROUND-2:
A sheet of taglines and a few background audios are provided and the team has to guess the correct brand.

ROUND-3:
This round has a quiz where the questions are based on brand logos.
