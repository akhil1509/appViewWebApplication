Tech by Chance

JUDGING CRITERIA : Participant who reach the highest no. on the chart.
DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
This round contains 25 basic technical questions on Electronics and Communications. Participants have to answer them within a time limit of 10 minutes. The short listed candidates will be qualified to next round.
ROUND-2:
It contains 1 dice having 6 numbers and one chart containing 1-50 no’s. In this game 1,3,5 are lucky numbers and if they get one of these numbers then the participants are free to move on chart & 2,4,6 are unlucky no’s and if participants get any of these numbers then they have to answer a technical question to move forward, if not the participants are supposed to stay at the same place. Similarly for 4 they will be asked two technical questions to move and for 6, three questions will be asked to move forward. The questions are based on electronic concepts. Participant who reaches highest number in chart will be declared as winner.
