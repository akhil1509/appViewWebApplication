DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
The participants will be provided an RC car and they need to figure out an exit to the maze. Top 8 contestants will be selected on the basis of time limit or maximum check points reached.

ROUND-2:
The game is going to be on one on one competition .This time they are provided with a maze which has two entries , The top 8 contestants are split into 4 pairs , one who reaches the centre of the maze first will be qualified to final round.

ROUND-3:
The participants will be given the same maze as round 2 but this time obstacles such as small blocks , sand etc. are kept to slow down the pace and has a time limit to declare the winner.
