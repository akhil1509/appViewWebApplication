Archer is Schloar




COST : 40/-
To register for the event contact Help desk at GRIET and Spot registrations are available
JUDGING CRITERIA : Based on number of correctly answered questions
DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
Registered participants are required to play the (archery/dart board). Depending on the target the participants hit, question is asked. The difficulty of the question increases as the target goes away from centre. For each strike 2 questions will be asked. For each question 5 marks will be awarded& one question will be technical & other non-technical. Time limit for each question is 30 sec. Participants with maximum marks in least time will be qualified to next round
ROUND-2:
In this round the qualified participants will be given 3 question for each strike. The time limit for each question is 45 sec. Top 2 of each group will be qualified to next round
ROUND-3:
In this round the qualified participants will be given 2 strikes & each strike will have 2 questions. The participant with the highest score will be declares as the winner.
Bonus points: In round 3, one who answers all answers correctly within 90 sec will be given bonus points
