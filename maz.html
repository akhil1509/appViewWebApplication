JUDGING CRITERIA : Based on maximum frame of questions solved in least time.
DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
Participants will be given two technical questions to solve. The obtained answers have to be reported to the volunteers. The team will be promoted to the next round if the answer is correct.
ROUND-2:
This round is similar to round 1, except that the difficulty level of the questions is increased. The team that answers both the questions will be provided with a third question. Team which solves maximum questions in least time frame will be declared as winners.
