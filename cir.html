JUDGING CRITERIA : Based on the number of correctly answered questions.
DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
Identification and Calculation of components. Resistor will be displayed and the participants have to identify and calculate them. The shortlisted participants will be qualified to next round.
ROUND-2:
Create a simple circuit. Participants have to draw any circuit by using the listed components. The Participants will be shortlisted to the next round based on the maximum no. of components used for creating a circuit.
ROUND-3:
Design your own circuit. The participant have to design the circuit in a given stipulated time. The individual with a innovative design of circuit will be declared as winner.
