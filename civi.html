JUDGING CRITERIA : Based on number of correctly answered questions.
DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
In this round the participants will have to answer 25 questions based on basic aptitude and current affairs in a given time frame where each question carries 1 mark. The shortlisted participants will be qualified to next round.
ROUND-2:
In this round the participants will be asked various questions related to civil engineering. The question will be posed according to their respective year. Participants who score maximum points will be qualified to next round.
ROUND-3:
Chits with alphabets written on it will be placed randomly Participants will have to pick two chits and based on the obtained alphabets, participants have to write technical words starting with all the letters (picked). Contestant who writes maximum marks in least span of time will be declared as winner.
