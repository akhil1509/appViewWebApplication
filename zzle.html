TechZZle




COST : 30/-
To register for the event contact Help desk at GRIET.
Spot registrations are available
JUDGING CRITERIA : Based on maximum number of questions answered in least time.
DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
Random letters will be given and maximum technical words should be written starting with the obtained letter within a given time. Participants who write maximum number of words will be short listed to next round.
ROUND-2:
A crossword puzzle will be given with technical and general questions and each of the team will be provided with the paper to solve. Team which solves the puzzle in least time will be short listed to next round.
ROUND-3:
This is a puzzle round in which the pictures related to electrical & technology are given to teams which are cut into 9 squares and are jumbled up. The team which identifies the correct picture first will be declared as the winner of the event.
