ROBO WRESTLING




COST : 40/-
To register for the event contact Help desk at GRIET and Spot registrations are available
JUDGING CRITERIA : Based on number of correctly answered questions
DESCRIPTION
 Robo Wrestling which is being one of the most excited Robo events from decades is again back. This time Robo Wrestling has exciting rounds. All the teams are categorized in to two groups A & B, one team from group A will compete with one team from group B, all the winners from group A & group B will be going to 2nd round. The wrestling will be upto 5 rounds depending upon the Number of teams signup.
Each round will have different arenas or at least some obstacles on the fixed arena.

Do’s and Do not’s
Only one person should operate the robot,
If the robot touches the boundary line, it will be considered as penalty,
If the robot falls out of the arena, then it is disqualified.
Batteries should be only placed on the robot, batteries built in remotes are disqualified.
Do not push or pull the robots with wire, it will be disqualified in such cases.
In case of any errors in the robots, time lapse of 3minutes will be given to rectify it, un identified problems beyond the time limit will be disqualified.
Any metal can be used to build the chassis of the robots.
There are no restrictions with the RPM, Torque, chassis or wheels of your robot.
Certificate of merit to winners and runner-up’s,
Certificate of participation to everyone who participated.
