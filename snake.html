JUDGING CRITERIA : The first team to finish wins. DESCRIPTION
DESCRIPTION
 Using a life sized snakes and ladders game field, participants will be able to play snakes and ladders game as the pieces on the board.
Each game will be of 6-7 games. Each team will have 2 members. Questions will be asked on the basics of ‘C’ and the difficulty of questions will increase after20-30 squares. When a team lands on snake, the team whose turn comes after them are allowed to choose the unseen questions. If the current team answers them correctly then they are not penalized for landing on snake. All other rules are as per classic snake and ladder game. First team to reach the end of the board is declared as winner
