JUDGING CRITERIA : Based on number of correctly answered questions and quality of the wall.
DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
A rapid fire round will be conducted where the participants will have to answer general multiple choice questions on building materials, brick bond ,aptitude and their scientific names. The shortlisted participants will be qualified to next round.
ROUND-2:
The participants are given bricks and other materials to build a wall of prescribed bond. The team which builds wall in least time will be declared as winner.
