Eco-QUIZ




COST : 30/-
To register for the event contact Help desk at GRIET.
Spot registrations are available.
JUDGING CRITERIA : Based on no. of correct answers in least time
DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
Pictures of different organisms, animals and plants will be displayed on slides for 15 seconds. The shortlisted participants are qualified to next round.
ROUND-2:
A crossword puzzle will be given based on questions related to environment. There is a time limit of 20 minutes. The shortlisted participants are qualified to next round.
ROUND-3:
3rd round consists of 2 rounds 1st round, we have rapid fire (1 min) for each team 2nd round there will be a quiz, 5 questions for each team. There is a pass question and negative marking in this round. At the end of 2 rounds, the scores will be added and a winner is decided.
