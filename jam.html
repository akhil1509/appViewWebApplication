JUDGING CRITERIA : Based on the performance of the individual .
DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
A group of 8 members are given a topic to discuss for 5 mins. The discussion should end in 4 minutes and conclude in the last 1 min. Based on the performance of all aspects of an individual few of them are shortlisted to next round.
ROUND-2:
A general topic will be given and the participants have to speak about the given topic for a minute. Based on the performance of all aspects of an individual few of them are shortlisted to next round.
ROUND-3:
A general topic will be given and the participants should convince the judge (or) should give the answers to the question asked by him. Based on the performance of all aspects of an individual the final winner will be declared.
