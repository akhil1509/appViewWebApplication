JUDGING CRITERIA : Based on points won
DESCRIPTION
 ROUND-1:
10 objects which are related to mechanical engineering are placed in certain order. The participants have to remember the order in which they are placed. The objects are then placed in random order. Now the participants need to place them in given order. Points are allotted based on how many are kept in perfect order. Based on points teams will be shortlisted for the next round
ROUND-2:
Random objects related to mechanical engineering are shown in a video. The participants needs to identify them and questions related to that object is asked and points will be awarded. Based on points, participants will be qualified to final round
ROUND-3:
In this round a passage related to Mechanical engineering is given. Contestant should read it in given time. Later questions related to that topic is asked. Points are allotted.
